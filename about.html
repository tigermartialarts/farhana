Welcome to Tiger Martial Arts

Located in the heart of Sharjah, UAE, Tiger Martial Arts is your ultimate destination for learning discipline, strength, and self-mastery through traditional and modern martial arts training. Our facility combines world-class instructors with a positive and empowering environment — ideal for students of all ages and skill levels.

We offer expert-led classes in Karate, Muay Thai, Judo, and Jiu-Jitsu — with specialized programs for kids, teens, and adults. Whether you’re training for fitness, competition, or personal growth, our community is here to support your journey. At Tiger Martial Arts, you’ll not only gain physical skills but also build resilience, focus, and confidence.

Join us and experience the spirit of martial arts rooted in respect, dedication, and excellence. We are conveniently located near Sharjah Industrial Area, with modern facilities, flexible class schedules, and certified black belt coaches ready to guide you. Your transformation begins here!
